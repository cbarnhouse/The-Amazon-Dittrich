About Dittrich

The Echo should have a personality that will lift you up when you're down, and give you an extra push when you need it the most. That's why we made Dittrich.

Over 1000 voice lines

Using a connection to the Direct Dittrich Delimiting Domain, we're able to give Dittrich a life-like personality. No one wants to feel like they're talking to a robot.

Be heard. Anywhere.

Dittrich can hear you from anywhere in the house. If you have multiple Dittrich's, you can play different music in each room, or the same song all across the house.
